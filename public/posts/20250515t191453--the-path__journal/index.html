The Path

May 15, 2025 Poem 

All is good; The path is well lit. There are steep inclines and rough terrain. But not to worry, the path paves the way.

In a single moment, it goes dark. Another moment goes by, and the path is gone. The incline grows steeper, and the terrain rougher.

The traveler sees a single light in the far off distance, and hopes the path will return. Another path has been paved, but it does not lead to the desired destination. The traveler is left with a choice.
