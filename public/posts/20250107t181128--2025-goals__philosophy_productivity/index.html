2025 Goals

Jan 7, 2025 Life 

Making goals is weird. They are really just an expression of what I want in the moment. There are genuine reasons that serve me as to why these goals would change. For example, I go back and forth on whether I want to read more, or play more games, or watch basketball. They all are hobbies that I want to do at night, but what I want always changes. Regardless, here are my 2025 goals:

Consume Marijuana and Alcohol on weekends only

I don’t think I will meet this goal, but the intent is that I get away from my current weed habits (which are nearing on daily). I also am considering just quitting alcohol all together, but we’ll see.

Read 12 books

Track on my Goodreads account

Resolve my ringing ear/dizziness issue

There is only so much control I have here, but I really want to fix this issue.

Complete my emacs jira project

I don’t even really know how bad I want this. Really what I want is just to prove to myself I can complete personal projects.

Continue to practice staying in the moment

This isn’t really a measurable goal. I could write ‘meditate 4 times a week’ or something, but I don’t necessarily think I have to do that to achieve what I want out of this.

The past couple weeks I have been better than I have been in a while, and especially recently I feel I have been very good at paying attention to the moment. Not holding on to anything, letting the good and the bad come as it may. As a result, I have noticed that certain things haven’t been as much of an issue, such as feeling terrible after work.
