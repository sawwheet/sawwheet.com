I have a question;  
to which there is no answer.  
Perhaps upon reflection,  
I will find direction.  

Beyond the door,  
the embrace of clarity.  
The teacher is fair,  
and she will listen.  

Outside is rain,  
a cloud of shame.  
I go back the way I came.  
Until next time Miss Jane.
